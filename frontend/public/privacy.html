Effective date: May 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ATS Resume Builder, operated by Nishanth Aradhya ("we," "us," or "our"), collects, uses, shares, stores, and deletes information when you use the ATS Resume Builder mobile app, website, backend services, and related features (together, the "Service").

Privacy contact: nishantharadhya7@gmail.com

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide, information created while using the Service, and information collected automatically by our app and service providers.

Account information

When you create or use an account, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Firebase user ID and authentication tokens
  • Sign-in provider information, including Google Sign-In if you choose to use it
  • Account plan, subscription status, account creation date, and last active date

Resume, profile, and job-search content

The app is designed to help you create resumes, cover letters, ATS checks, job applications, and related job-search materials. Depending on the features you use, we may collect and store:

  • Resume titles, templates, color preferences, sections, version history, target roles, target job descriptions, ATS scores, and download counts
  • Personal resume details you enter, such as name, email, phone number, location, LinkedIn/profile links, summary, skills, education, experience, projects, certifications, awards, and languages
  • Cover letters, company names, generated text, word counts, and related metadata
  • Job tracker information, including companies, roles, application status, notes, and applied dates
  • Profile photos or other images you choose to upload
  • PDF or DOCX files generated or uploaded through the Service
  • LinkedIn profile or LinkedIn export data if you choose to import it

Please do not include sensitive information in your resume or job-search content unless it is necessary for your own use of the Service.

Files, photos, and device permissions

The app may request permission to access photos, media, or files only when needed for user-selected features such as uploading a profile photo, importing a LinkedIn ZIP export, or saving/sharing a resume file. The app does not access your files or photos in the background.

AI feature inputs and outputs

When you use AI features such as ATS analysis, keyword matching, bullet improvement, resume tailoring, summary generation, or cover letter generation, we process the text you submit and the AI-generated output. This may include resume text, job descriptions, company names, target roles, and other content you provide.

Usage, diagnostics, and device information

We and our service providers may collect:

  • App interactions, feature usage, and usage limits
  • Crash logs, diagnostics, app performance data, and error reports
  • Device type, operating system, app version, language, approximate region, and network-related information
  • Advertising identifiers or similar device identifiers when ads are shown
  • Local preferences stored on your device, such as theme setting, onboarding state, and local usage counters

Payment and subscription information

If paid features are enabled, purchases are processed by Google Play and/or subscription service providers such as RevenueCat. We do not collect or store full payment card numbers. We may receive and store subscription status, product identifiers, purchase/entitlement status, renewal state, and related transaction metadata needed to provide paid features and support purchases.

  • Generate ATS scores, summaries, tailored resumes, improved bullets, keyword matches, and cover letters

  • Generate and share resume files or share links when you choose to use those features
  • Provide free, ad-supported, and paid features

  • Monitor app reliability, fix bugs, improve performance, and maintain security
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, unauthorized access, and policy violations
  • Respond to support requests, legal requests, and user deletion requests
  • Comply with applicable laws and Google Play policies

We do not sell your personal and sensitive user data.

The Service uses AI to assist with resume and career-document writing. AI outputs may be inaccurate, incomplete, outdated, or inappropriate. You are responsible for reviewing, editing, and verifying all AI-generated content before using it in job applications or sharing it with others.

We use safeguards and input validation to reduce unsafe or unsupported outputs, but no AI system is perfect. You can report offensive, unsafe, misleading, or otherwise problematic AI output by contacting nishantharadhya7@gmail.com or using the in-app reporting option.
